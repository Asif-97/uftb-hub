AI-Powered Student Support System

Abstract

This project introduces an AI-powered support system designed to assist students with their academic queries. The system utilizes a sophisticated chatbot that leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand and respond to student questions in real-time, covering topics from course-specific material to university administrative procedures. The platform aims to reduce the workload on faculty advisors and provide students with immediate, 24/7 assistance, thereby enhancing the overall educational experience.
